Locked UX decisions from 04-CONTEXT.md:
- Scan belongs in the add-item area on the list page, not buried in settings.
- The scan result should feel like the existing bottom-sheet flows from Phase 3.
- Camera scan and manual EAN are two entry methods into one lookup-and-confirm tool.

Scanner constraints from 04-RESEARCH.md:
- Use `html5-qrcode` low-level API or equivalent polyfill-backed path, not the stock widget.
- Restrict to `EAN_13`, `EAN_8`, `UPC_A`, `UPC_E`.
- Require explicit user action to open camera.
- Stop tracks on success, dismiss, route change, and `visibilitychange`.
- Always render manual EAN fallback.
